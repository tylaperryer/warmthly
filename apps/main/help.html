Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Donations

How do I make a donation?

To make a donation, click the "Donate" button on our homepage. You'll be able to choose a preset amount or enter a custom amount. We accept multiple currencies and use secure payment processing through Yoco.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor, Yoco. All payments are processed securely and we never store your full payment details.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Tax deductibility depends on your location and local tax laws. Please consult with a tax professional in your jurisdiction. We provide receipts for all donations that you can use for tax purposes.

Can I donate in different currencies?

Yes! We support multiple currencies. You can select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu in the donation form. The amount will be converted at the current exchange rate.

How is my donation used?

We maintain complete transparency about how donations are used. You can view all transactions and decisions on our Mint page, which shows real-time tracking of every donation and how it's allocated. See our complete organizational timeline on our Post page.

Transparency & Tracking

How does Warmthly ensure transparency?

We believe transparency is fundamental to trust. Every donation is tracked and displayed on our Mint page in real-time. All organizational decisions and milestones are recorded on our Post page, creating a complete timeline of our activities.

Can I see where my specific donation went?

Yes! All donations are tracked individually on the Mint page. While we maintain privacy by not displaying personal information, you can see the flow of funds and how they're allocated to different initiatives.

What is the Mint page?

The Mint page is our live transaction tracker. It shows every donation in real-time and provides complete transparency into how funds are received and allocated. It's our commitment to making every cent visible.

What is the Post page?

The Post page is our organizational timeline. It records every decision, milestone, and community interaction, creating a living history of our organization's story and ensuring complete transparency.

About Warmthly

What is Warmthly?

Warmthly is a global movement to make empathy a measurable part of our systems. We work to rehumanize our world by creating transparent, accountable systems that prioritize human connection and understanding. Learn more on our homepage or see our privacy policy.

What does "Rehumanize Our World" mean?

"Rehumanize Our World" is our mission statement. It represents our commitment to bringing human connection, empathy, and understanding back into systems that have become impersonal or disconnected. We believe that by making empathy measurable and transparent, we can create better outcomes for everyone.

How can I get involved beyond donating?

There are many ways to get involved! You can share our mission, report concerns or suggestions on our Post Report page, participate in community decisions, or simply help spread awareness about the importance of transparency and empathy in our systems.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble making a donation. What should I do?

If you're experiencing issues with the donation process, please try:

  • Refreshing the page and trying again
  • Checking that your payment information is correct
  • Ensuring you have a stable internet connection
  • Contacting us at desk@warmthly.org if the problem persists
The website isn't loading properly. What can I do?

We've designed our website to work on all modern browsers. If you're experiencing issues:

  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Check that JavaScript is enabled
  • If problems persist, please contact us
Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS), secure payment processing that meets PCI DSS standards, and we never store your full payment details. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.

Do you use cookies or tracking?

We use minimal, essential cookies only for functionality (like remembering your preferences). We do not use third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising cookies. Your privacy is important to us. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Accessibility

Is the website accessible?

Yes! We're committed to making Warmthly accessible to everyone. Our website meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards and includes:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast support
  • Responsive design that works at all zoom levels
  • Clear focus indicators
  • Semantic HTML structure
  • Multiple reading levels (Standard, Simplified, Easy Read)
  • Sign language videos and transcripts for media

If you encounter any accessibility issues, please let us know.

Can I adjust text size or spacing?

Yes! You can use your browser's zoom feature (Ctrl/Cmd + Plus to zoom in, Ctrl/Cmd + Minus to zoom out) to adjust text size. Our website is designed to work well at up to 400% zoom. We also have reading level controls that let you choose Standard, Simplified (Grade 6), or Easy Read formats.
