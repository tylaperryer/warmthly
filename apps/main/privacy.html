Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Warmthly, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Donation Information: When you make a donation, we collect payment information (processed securely through Yoco) and optional information like your name and email if you choose to provide it.
  • Communication: If you contact us via email or our contact forms, we collect the information you provide.
  • Technical Data: We automatically collect basic technical information (IP address, browser type) for security and functionality purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Process donations and maintain transparency records
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

What We Don't Do

We do not:

  • Track you across websites
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your information except as required by law
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • Secure payment processing (Yoco) that meets PCI DSS standards
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure (Cloudflare)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at desk@warmthly.org.

Error Tracking

We use client-side error tracking that stores errors locally in your browser. This information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to report an error. This helps us fix bugs and improve the user experience.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Yoco: Payment processing (see their privacy policy)
  • Resend: Email delivery (see their privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare: Website hosting and security (see their privacy policy)

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: desk@warmthly.org
Website: www.warmthly.org
