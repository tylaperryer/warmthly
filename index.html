Rehumanize Our World.

Warmthly is a global movement to make empathy a measurable part of our systems.

Our Method

The room is part of the treatment. A stressful room creates stressed patients. Our method is designed to prove this and make our systems more human, one space at a time.

1. Redesign with Empathy

We partner with institutions to introduce small, evidence-based environmental changes. By improving factors like light, color, and personalization, we create spaces that feel safer, calmer, and more human.

2. Measure the impact

This isn't just about feeling good; it's about data. Using surveys and analysis, we measure the impact of our changes on stress levels and user satisfaction, providing concrete proof that empathy-first design works.

3. Share the Knowledge

We publish our findings and methodologies as open-source "playbooks." This allows anyone, anywhere, to replicate our success, adapt our methods, and contribute to a more human-centered world.
